Important Screening and Workshop Ticketing Process for 2015 Flyway Film Festival Pass Holders

As 2015 Flyway Pass Holders, you get tickets FIRST for any film screening during the festival run, as well as priority seating at each of our screening venues when you arrive at least 15 minutes prior to the event.

Note: All passes are non-transferable and non-refundable. Only one ticket may be booked per screening or event, per pass.

To guarantee admission, all event-ticketed Pass Holders MUST be present in line at the venue no later than 15 minutes prior to published screening time and be prepared to show your pass and pre-booked ticket (either your printed ticket or the Universe confirmation on your mobile device).

Priority access for all event-ticketed Pass Holders expires 15 minutes prior to start of screening or event. After that time, all event-ticketed Pass Holders will be asked to join the regular ticket holder line for seating.

Online Pre-Booking Tickets for Pass Holders

To ensure the best possibility of you being able to see all of the movies you’d like to see during the run of the Festival, we encourage you to pre-book all of your screening tickets online, in advance of the Festival. Here is how to pre-book your screening or event tickets:

  • Visit http://flywayfilmfestival.org/2015/films/ to review our 2015 schedule and film selections.
  • Select a film you would like to see or event you would like to attend and click on the ticket icon to the left of the screening day and time. This will take you to the Universe ticketing page for the screening or event. NOTE: bookings must be currently done one movie at a time, as there is no “shopping cart” option.
  • Click on the black Buy Tickets Now button, then click the blue hyperlink on the left side of the window that says “Have an access key?”
  • Enter your Access Key: Your Access Key is the pass holder’s first and last names with no space in between (as entered during the pass purchase process). Enter your first and last name together (no spaces, not case-sensitive) (example: johnnydepp) and hit “” Select 1 ticket from the dropdown quantity field. NOTE: Only 1 ticket may be selected per access key/pass holder name per film or event.
  • Provide your first and last name and email address in the required Passholder Ticket fields (Critical: this will confirm that your name will appear on the event guest list so that you can check in at the door with or without a printed ticket), then select the Sign In tab at the top right of the screen and enter your Universe user name, email and password (you set this up when you purchased your Festival passes). You will only have to log in to Universe once if you are choosing multiple event tickets.
  • Click the blue “Confirm Booking” button. You will get a confirmation screen.
  • Check your email for an email from Universe that will be titled: Confirmation Receipt: You Booked (Film Title or Event) email that will let you print the PDF of your reserved ticket (welcome, but not necessary if you have followed the process above) or allow you to show it to venue staff on your mobile device – and that’s it! Remember to check your spam/junk/promotions mail folders if you do not see it within a reasonable amount of time!

In-person Pre-Booking or Same-Day Tickets for Pass Holders

Beginning October 20, you may visit our Main Festival Office (404 First Street in Pepin, WI) during regular posted hours, or visit any of our venue Box Offices during the Festival, October 21-25, up to one hour prior to the scheduled screening start time. Ticketing volunteers can help you book and print tickets for any screening that has tickets still available. Advance ticketing ends one hour prior to each event.

  • It is helpful if you have pre-selected which screening or event you would like to attend.
  • You will need to show your festival pass to Box Office staff and provide your fist and last name.
  • If Advance Tickets are still available for your selected screening or event, you may book one ticket per screening/event per pass, and receive a printed ticket.
  • To guarantee screening admission, you must be present in line with your pass at the venue 15 minutes prior to the scheduled screening start time.

If you are unable to obtain an Advance Ticket to your screening of choice, or within 1 hour prior to any screening, you may choose to be Wait Listed. See more information about our 2015 Wait List process and policies below.

Wait List Ticket Process
Screenings and events that have no more Advance Tickets available will be listed as SOLD OUT and indicated as “Wait List.” SOLD OUT means that all tickets for a film have been purchased or pre-booked. Wait List tickets will be available 5-10 minutes prior to screenings when reserved ticket holders have not presented prior to the 15 minute priority seating period. NOTE: There is a one (1) Wait List Ticket limit per person. Admission is not guaranteed.

  • Beginning 1 hour (no earlier) prior to the SOLD OUT screening, Wait List patrons can visit the screening venue Box Office to receive a Wait List number (one number per person).
  • With your number obtained, you are free to leave the venue, if desired.

15 minutes prior to the start of the screening or event, Wait List patrons should resume their place in line with their number in hand. If there are 5 seats left after all advance ticketholders are seated, 5 Wait List tickets will be released and Wait List numbers 1-5 will be admitted. Pass Holders will receive free admission via the Wait List (must have Pass with you); non-Pass Holders pay at time of Wait List admission.
