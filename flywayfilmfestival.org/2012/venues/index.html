Lounge

2012 Flyway Film Festival Hospitality Lounge at The Breakwater
At the corner of 1st & Main, Pepin, Wisconsin

Overlooking the harbor, this is the official festival hangout to see and be seen. Open to VIP’s, Sponsors and Passholders during the daytime hours (9am-2pm) and open to the public as the place to meet and mingle with fellow film lovers and international filmmakers during the evening. (Beer and Wine are available for purchase, must be of legal drinking age and provide a valid state issued ID).

  • Info

    The Seventh Annual 2012 Flyway Film Festival will take place October 23-26, 2014 in Pepin & Stockholm, Wisconsin. The 2012 Flyway Film Festival was founded by Rick Vaicius in 2008 on the beautiful shores of Lake Pepin on the Mississippi River. Wildlife abounds with songbirds, tundra swans, diving ducks, dabbling ducks, bald eagles, otter, white-tailed deer, and more, all thriving along this majestic migratory route.
