EgoFest 2011 Wrap

Mar 20, 2011
By Rick
News
One Comment
Just a few hours removed from EgoFest, the lovely folks up in Brainerd, Minnesota – Phil Holbrook et al, and once again walked away feeling that the indie film community is alive and well.It was great to see so many Flyway Alums and meet many really talented filmmakers, like Phil Seneker, Jennifer Peepas, and Mario Contreras, among others, that we’ve been recently connected to via Twitter.

A number of films may find their way to Flyway come October, so stay tuned.

A big thanks to Phil Holbrook for doing such a great job putting together a great event, and thanks to Julie and Jessica (@Kingisafink) for tolerating yours truly for another weekend of film and carousing.

2011 Flyway Trailer Contest

Mar 15, 2011
By Rick
News
One Comment

We are extremely happy to announce our 2011 Flyway Trailer Contest. Winner(s) will receive a check for $250 and a copy of Final Draft and Sony Vegas Pro 10!

There are two trailer categories for consideration:

  • 30-seconds in length
  • 60-seconds in length

Submissions can be made in either category or in both categories.

Trailers may be funny, serious or experimental. We encourage you to include elements which promote independent film, filmmakers and Flyway’s location on Lake Pepin and the Upper Mississippi River. We leave it to you as to how to interpret those elements. Deadline is July 31st, 2011. Download your entry form and guidelines here.

2011 Call For Entries Deadline Extended to August 1

Jan 30, 2011
By Rick
Blog, News
One Comment

Because our traditional July 31 deadline for entries falls today, on Sunday, we’ve decided to extend the deadline for postmarking your entry to Monday, August 1, 2011.

We hope you will take advantage of the extension and send your entry in.

 

The Fourth Annual 2011 Flyway Film Festival will take place October 20-23, 2011.

If you are a filmmaker and wish to submit your feature-length, short-film or student-film (narrative or documentary) for consideration, please download and follow the instructions in this FFF Entry Form PDF. Deadline for entry is July 31, 2011.

  • Info

    The Seventh Annual 2011 Flyway Film Festival will take place October 23-26, 2014 in Pepin & Stockholm, Wisconsin. The 2011 Flyway Film Festival was founded by Rick Vaicius in 2008 on the beautiful shores of Lake Pepin on the Mississippi River. Wildlife abounds with songbirds, tundra swans, diving ducks, dabbling ducks, bald eagles, otter, white-tailed deer, and more, all thriving along this majestic migratory route.
