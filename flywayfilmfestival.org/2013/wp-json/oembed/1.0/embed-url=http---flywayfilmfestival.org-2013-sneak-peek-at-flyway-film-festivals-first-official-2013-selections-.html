  • Info

    The Seventh Annual 2013 Flyway Film Festival will take place October 23-26, 2014 in Pepin & Stockholm, Wisconsin. The 2013 Flyway Film Festival was founded by Rick Vaicius in 2008 on the beautiful shores of Lake Pepin on the Mississippi River. Wildlife abounds with songbirds, tundra swans, diving ducks, dabbling ducks, bald eagles, otter, white-tailed deer, and more, all thriving along this majestic migratory route.
