2016 Festival Kickoff Gala

The 2016 Festival opens on Wednesday, October 19th with the annual Kickoff Gala at the Villa Bellezza Winery in Pepin. Featuring food, libations, music, a keynote address, and the Festival “Ax” awards ceremony. The evening begins at 6:00pm with an array of drinks and hors d’oeuvres catered by local restaurateurs.



Opening Night Film

Girl Asleep

Join us Thursday, Oct 20, 2016, 7:30 pm in the Villa Bellezza Vat Room for “Girl, Asleep,” a new Australian film that’s got a bit of Michel Gondry, Spike Jones, and Napoleon Dynamite all mixed up in a teen fever dream.



Closing Night Film

American Fable

Wrap up the fest on Sunday, Oct 23, 2016, 5:00 pm at the Widespot Performing Arts Center for our Closing Night Film. AMERICAN FABLE is a fairy-tale thriller set in the 1980s Midwest farm crisis about a courageous girl living in a dark and sometimes magical world.
