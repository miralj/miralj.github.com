Flyway 2016 Venues

Big River Theater

Big River Theater

Join us at the Big River Theater in Alma for screenings.

121 S Main St, Alma, WI 54610, USA

Villa Bellezza

Villa Bellezza Vat Room

The Villa is all Italian, constructed from the finest imported elements, and nestled on the fertile land of the riverside. Taste some of the locally-made wine and tour the vineyards while you are here. The 80+ seat Vat Room is a new venue this year.

1400-1560 Great River Road, Pepin, WI 54759, USA

Widespot

Widespot Performing Arts Center

Conveniently located right upstairs from the Stockholm Pie Factory (you do NOT want to miss out on those baked goods!), this old music hall is a cultural hub of the River Road.

N2030 Spring St. (2nd floor), Stockholm, WI 54769

Sheldon Theatre

Sheldon Theatre

Built in 1904 and renovated in 1988, this 466-seat theater is a gem of southern Minnesota. It even features a rare Kilgen pipe organ!

443 West 3rd Street, Red Wing, MN 55066 USA

Lounge

Filmmaker Lounge @ Minema

The lounge this year is up one block from the waterfront of Lake Pepin. Someday it shall reopen as the new Flyway Minema.

408 2nd Street, Pepin, WI 54759, USA

Dougieland Studio

Flyway Box Office

Dougieland Studio is the Flyway 2016 Box Office and Store. Stop by for a visit!

Wednesday–Saturday: 10am–6pm
Sunday: 10am–3pm

2nd and Main, Pepin, WI, 54759
