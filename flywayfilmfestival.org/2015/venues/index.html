Flyway 2015 Venues

Minema

Flyway Minema Festival Lounge

This 40-seat micro cinema is a cozy and comfy hangout space right on the waterfront of Lake Pepin. The filmmaker’s lounge is a hangout space and open venue for visiting filmmakers, speakers, volunteers, and staff to catch some down-time and have deep (or not) conversations and refreshments.

406 1st Street, Pepin, WI 54759, USA

Villa Bellezza

Villa Bellezza

The Villa is all Italian, constructed from the finest imported elements, and nestled on the fertile land of the riverside. Taste some of the locally-made wine and tour the vineyards while you are here.

1400-1560 Great River Road, Pepin, WI 54759, USA

Widespot

Widespot Performing Arts Center

Conveniently located right upstairs from the Stockholm Pie Factory (you do NOT want to miss out on those baked goods!), this old music hall is a cultural hub of the River Road.

N2030 Spring St. (2nd floor), Stockholm, WI 54769

Big River Theater

Big River Theater

Join us at the Big River Theater in Alma for screenings.

121 S Main St, Alma, WI 54610, USA

Maiden Rock Inn

Maiden Rock Inn

The Maiden Rock inn is nestled into the bluff, just two blocks from the shore of Lake Pepin in the historic village of Maiden Rock, Wisconsin.

N531 County Road South, Maiden Rock, WI 54750, USA

St. James Hotel

St. James Hotel

The St. James Hotel opened on Thanksgiving Day, 1875, after a group of 11 civic-minded businessmen recognized the need for a first-class lodging establishment in the port city of Red Wing, Minnesota, the wheat-trading center of the world.

406 Main Street, Red Wing, MN 55066, USA

Info

The Eighth Annual Flyway Film Festival will take place October 21-25, 2015 in Pepin, Stockholm, and Alma, Wisconsin, with events in Red Wing, Minnesota. The Flyway Film Festival was founded by Rick Vaicius in 2008 on the beautiful shores of Lake Pepin on the Mississippi River. Wildlife abounds with songbirds, tundra swans, diving ducks, dabbling ducks, bald eagles, otter, white-tailed deer, and more, all thriving along this majestic migratory route.
