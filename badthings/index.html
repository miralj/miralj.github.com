work-in-progress // a short film // by Mira K. Lippold-Johnson

STORY

When her older sister gets suspended, an 8-year-old girl on scholarship at an elite private school starts to doubt that hard work is all it takes to get ahead.

ABOUT

Bad Things is a graduate MFA thesis film currently in production at the University of Texas at Austin.
