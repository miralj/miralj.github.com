An award-winning short film about realizing that the world isn't fair.

USA | 2018 | 22min

Written & Directed by Mira K. Lippold-Johnson
Starring Calah Lane, Nya Garner and Joe Colaleo
Cinematography by Rachel Bardin | Produced by Sarah Hennigan

- John Wildman, Festworks
