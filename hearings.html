The Hearings

(A Kavanaugh Short Film)

In the days following Christine Blasey Ford's testimony, we shot this as a way of documenting the frustration of the moment. It's a short film about being female, about being angry, and about the need for a room of one's own.

Starring Kim Tran and Jonathan Flanders
Directed by Mira K. Lippold-Johnson
Story by Mira K. Lippold-Johnson and Sophia Loffreda
Cinematography by Rachel Bardin
Produced by Joe Lee
Production Design: Daniel Earney
Location Sound: Matthew Reid Kluchin
Camera Assistant: Maria Fernandes Flores
Special Thanks to Jo Huang-Zollner
