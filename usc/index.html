SHE WANTED TO FLY


In her dreams she flew.
Every night, when she closed her eyes, she would wake up in a world where she had the power of flight. She would simply take a step and her body would float into the sky.


And every morning, she'd wake up sure that if she tried hard enough, she could learn to fly in the waking world as well.

But she'd take a step and instead of finding weightlessness, she remained glued to the ground.


Over time she got more creative, trying everything she could think of to propel herself into the sky.


Nothing worked.



So she stopped waking up in the morning.


She stayed in bed and spent her days and nights in the world of weightlessness and freedom.

She was happy there. She flew and flew, higher and higher into the emptiness above the sky.


Until one day she realized she’d lost track of the ground.
All around her was nothingness and she couldn’t tell where she was going.
Was she flying upwards? Falling down? Standing still? She called out for help, but there was no one.


When she woke up, she was glad to have the ground under her feet.
