A Los Angeles based filmmaker whose work centers around the internal struggles of courageous women.

Mira works for the University of Texas at Austin as an Assistant Professor of Practice and Director of the UTLA 'Semester in Los Angeles' program. She has been awarded an Austin Film Society grant for her work and has won awards at Sidewalk Film Festival, Women Texas Film Festival, Bend Film Festival, and Austin Revolution Film Festival.

At one point she was the supervisor of digital media content at the Smithsonian Channel and at another created a zine/book of cocktail recipes. She holds a bachelor's degree in linguistics from Harvard University and an MFA in film production from the University of Texas.
